# KIRA
App that helps international students w/ high school in U.S.A
Overview
KIRA is a mobile application designed to help international high school students in the U.S. plan their academic and career paths in a structured and personalized way. It turns overwhelming college and career information into clear, step-by-step roadmaps that students can follow directly from their phones.
Problem
International high school students often struggle to understand how their current choices in high school connect to future careers and college admissions. Information about majors, extracurriculars, scholarships, and opportunities is scattered across many platforms, making it difficult to plan effectively.
Solution
KIRA centralizes this information into a mobile-first platform that provides personalized career pathways and academic planning tools. Students can explore careers, track progress, and follow structured guidance tailored to their goals.
Key Features
Career Path Explorer with personalized roadmaps
College Admissions Planning dashboard
Scholarship discovery section
Opportunity hub (internships, programs, competitions)
Student profile tracker (GPA, activities, achievements)
Resume-building support
Platform
Mobile-only application (iOS + Android)
Built for on-the-go access and daily student use
Tech Stack
React Native (Expo recommended)
Firebase (authentication & backend)
Firestore (database)
GitHub (version control)
Goal
The goal of KIRA is to make academic and career planning simple, structured, and accessible for international high school students by delivering personalized guidance directly through a mobile app.
